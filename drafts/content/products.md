# Products

## Garand-101 Magnetometer-Gradiometer

The Garand-101 represents our flagship product – a state-of-the-art magnetometer-gradiometer designed for professional geophysical surveys and research applications.

### Key Features

- **Dual-sensor gradiometer configuration** for superior noise rejection
- **Ultra-high sensitivity** with resolution better than 0.001 nT
- **Portable design** weighing only 2.3 kg
- **Long battery life** – up to 12 hours continuous operation
- **Weather-resistant construction** for field deployment
- **Real-time data display** with integrated GPS positioning

### Technical Specifications

- **Sensor Type**: Fluxgate magnetometer
- **Measurement Range**: ±100,000 nT
- **Gradient Measurement**: Up to 10,000 nT/m
- **Accuracy**: ±0.1 nT (absolute), ±0.01 nT (gradient)
- **Sampling Rate**: 10 Hz continuous
- **Operating Temperature**: -40°C to +70°C
- **Data Storage**: Internal 32 GB, USB export

### Applications

- Mineral exploration and mining surveys
- Archaeological investigations
- Environmental monitoring and contamination studies
- Geological research and mapping
- Pipeline and cable detection
- Unexploded ordnance (UXO) detection

### Package Includes

- Garand-101 sensor unit with protective case
- Data acquisition tablet with specialized software
- Rechargeable battery pack and charger
- Calibration certificate and user manual
- Transportation case with custom foam inserts
