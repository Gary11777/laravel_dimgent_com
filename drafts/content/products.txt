Garand-101 Magnetometer-Gradiometer

The Garand-101 is our flagship magnetometer-gradiometer system, designed for high-precision magnetic field measurements. This advanced instrument combines cutting-edge sensor technology with robust field deployment capabilities.

Key Features:
- High sensitivity magnetic field detection
- Dual-sensor gradiometer configuration
- Portable and field-ready design
- Real-time data acquisition and processing
- Advanced noise filtering and signal processing
- Durable construction for harsh environments
- User-friendly interface and software

Applications:
- Mineral exploration and mining
- Archaeological surveys
- Environmental monitoring
- Geological mapping
- Pipeline and infrastructure inspection
- Unexploded ordnance detection

Technical Specifications:
- Measurement range: ±100,000 nT
- Resolution: 0.1 nT
- Sampling rate: Up to 20 Hz
- Operating temperature: -20°C to +50°C
- Power supply: Rechargeable battery pack
- Data storage: Internal memory + external storage options
